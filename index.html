About Us

Welcome to Chanchal Physiotherapy Clinic, located in the heart of Hajipur, Bihar. Our mission is to provide high-quality physiotherapy treatments tailored to your specific needs. With years of experience, modern techniques, and state-of-the-art equipment, we are dedicated to helping you regain your mobility and lead a healthier, pain-free life.

Let us help you take the first step towards a better quality of life. Visit us and experience the difference!

Our Services

We offer a variety of physiotherapy treatments designed to restore your health and improve your quality of life.

Paralysis Treatment

Specialized care for restoring mobility and functionality (लकवा).

Sciatica Treatment

Relief for nerve pain and associated discomfort (सायटिका).

Polio Treatment

Supportive therapy for strengthening and recovery (पोलियो).

Arthritis Treatment

Advanced care for joint pain and inflammation (गठिया).

Back Pain Treatment

Effective solutions for a healthier, pain-free back (कमर दर्द).

Additional Treatments

Includes stiffness, swelling, bandage, traction, and more.
